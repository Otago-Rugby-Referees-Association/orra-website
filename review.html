Referee Review Platform

The ORRA Referee Review Platform is a tool for continuous improvement, providing structured feedback and performance assessment for all referees.

Purpose of the Review Platform

The review platform serves several important functions:

  • Track individual referee development over time
  • Identify strengths and areas for improvement
  • Provide constructive feedback from coaches and assessors
  • Support appointment and promotion decisions
  • Guide training and mentoring programs
  • Ensure consistent officiating standards

Review Process

Match Observation

Selected matches are observed by qualified referee coaches or assessors who evaluate performance across multiple criteria:

  • Law application and accuracy
  • Positioning and fitness
  • Communication with players and team management
  • Match management and control
  • Decision-making consistency
  • Professionalism and presentation

Feedback Delivery

Following observation, referees receive:

  • Immediate verbal feedback post-match when possible
  • Written report within 7 days
  • Video analysis where available
  • Development recommendations
  • Follow-up discussions as needed

Performance Criteria

Reviews assess performance across key competency areas:

Area Key Elements
Law Knowledge Accurate application, consistency, current interpretations
Positioning Field position, angles, following play, fitness
Communication Clarity, player management, conflict resolution
Match Control Authority, consistency, tempo management
Decision Making Accuracy, speed, confidence, materiality
Professionalism Presentation, punctuality, conduct, administration

Review Frequency

The frequency of formal reviews depends on referee level and development stage:

  • Developing Referees: Every 3-4 matches in first two seasons
  • Experienced Referees: Minimum 2-3 formal reviews per season
  • High Performance: Multiple reviews throughout season plus video analysis
  • Promotional Assessment: Additional reviews when being considered for advancement

Accessing Your Reviews

Review Portal

Referees can access their performance reviews through the ORRA member portal. Contact the Development Officer for login credentials and platform access.

Using Feedback Effectively

To get the most from your reviews:

  • Read feedback carefully and objectively
  • Focus on specific, actionable recommendations
  • Discuss unclear points with your coach or mentor
  • Set goals based on areas for improvement
  • Track progress over multiple reviews
  • Request video analysis for complex situations
  • Maintain a growth mindset and embrace learning opportunities

Self-Assessment

In addition to formal reviews, referees are encouraged to:

  • Keep a match journal noting key decisions and situations
  • Review their own performances honestly
  • Seek informal feedback from experienced colleagues
  • Identify patterns in their officiating
  • Set personal development goals

Video Analysis

Match Recording

Where match footage is available, video analysis provides valuable insights into:

  • Positioning and angles
  • Decision accuracy with multiple replays
  • Communication style and effectiveness
  • Pattern recognition in officiating
  • Body language and presence

Appeals and Concerns

If you have concerns about a review or assessment:

  • Discuss directly with the assessing coach first
  • Contact the Development Officer for clarification
  • Request a second opinion if needed
  • Follow the formal appeals process outlined in ORRA policies

Continuous Improvement

The review platform is part of ORRA's commitment to referee development. We believe that constructive feedback, combined with quality training and mentoring, enables all referees to reach their potential.

Questions About Reviews?

For questions about the review platform, your assessments, or how to improve your performance, contact the Development Officer.
