About ORRA

The Otago Rugby Referees Association Incorporated serves as the primary organization for rugby referees in the Otago region. We are dedicated to:

  • Developing referee skills and knowledge through comprehensive training programs
  • Supporting referees at all levels of the game
  • Maintaining high standards of officiating in Otago rugby
  • Fostering a supportive and inclusive referee community
  • Providing resources and guidance to help referees succeed
