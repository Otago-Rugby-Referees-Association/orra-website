To add your photos to this gallery:

  1. Save your photo files to the images/referees/, images/events/, or images/sponsors/ folders
  2. Recommended size: 600x800px (portrait) or 800x600px (landscape)
  3. Supported formats: JPG, PNG
  4. Edit this HTML file and replace the placeholder divs with:
    <div class="photo-item"><img src="images/referees/your-photo.jpg" alt="Description"><div class="photo-caption">Photo caption here</div></div>
