Fuel Reimbursements

Reimbursement Policy

ORRA provides fuel reimbursements to referees who travel to matches. Claims must be submitted within 30 days of the match date.

How to Claim

  1. Complete the fuel reimbursement form (available from the treasurer)
  2. Include match details: date, venue, teams, and distance traveled
  3. Submit to the treasurer via email or in person
  4. Reimbursements are processed monthly

Reimbursement Rates

Distance (km) Rate per km Notes
All distances $0.50 Standard IRD rate

Important Notes

  • Distance is calculated from your home address to the venue and return
  • Car-pooling is encouraged - contact the appointments officer
  • Keep records of all matches for tax purposes
  • Contact the treasurer if you have questions about your claim

Contact for Claims

For questions about fuel reimbursements, please contact the Treasurer.
