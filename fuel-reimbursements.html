Fuel Reimbursements

Reimbursement Policy

ORRA provides fuel reimbursements to referees who travel to matches. Claims must be submitted within 30 days of the match date using the Google Form below.

Reimbursement Rates

Distance Rate per km Notes
All distances $0.79/km Standard IRD rate (subject to annual review)

Important Notes

  • Distance is calculated from your home address to the venue and return
  • Car-pooling is encouraged - please coordinate with regional reps
  • Keep records of all matches for tax purposes
  • Claims processed monthly - allow 2-3 weeks for payment
  • Receipts required for expenses over $50
