Appointment Process

Appointments are made based on several factors:

  • Referee availability and preferences
  • Match grade and importance
  • Development opportunities
  • Geographic location and travel requirements
  • Performance and fitness levels
  • Conflicts of interest (club affiliations)

Submitting Your Availability

Monthly Availability

Please submit your availability for each month by the 25th of the preceding month. This allows the appointments officer to create an effective draw that accommodates referee preferences while ensuring quality officiating for all matches.

What to Do When Appointed

When you receive a match appointment:

  • Confirm receipt and acceptance of the appointment
  • Note the match details in your calendar
  • Review the teams and any specific considerations
  • Arrange travel and allow adequate arrival time
  • Confirm your attendance 48 hours before kickoff
  • If unable to attend, notify the appointments officer immediately

Late Changes

If you can no longer fulfill an appointment:

  • Contact the appointments officer as soon as possible
  • Provide clear reasons for the withdrawal
  • Be aware that late withdrawals may affect future appointments

Match Day Procedures

Pre-Match

  • Arrive at least 45 minutes before kickoff
  • Check playing surface and facilities
  • Meet with assistant referees if appointed
  • Conduct captains' meeting
  • Complete equipment checks

Post-Match

  • Complete match report within 24 hours
  • Submit any incident reports if required
  • Note yellow/red cards in the system
  • Provide feedback to appointments officer if needed

Questions About Appointments?

For any questions about the draw or your appointments, contact the Appointments Officer.
