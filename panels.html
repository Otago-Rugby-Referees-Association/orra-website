Panel Selection and Progression

Panel placements are reviewed annually by the executive committee in consultation with the Coaching & Development officer. Progression is based on:

  • Meeting or exceeding fitness standards (Bronco test)
  • Achieving required law test results
  • Consistent match performance
  • Assessment scores and feedback
  • Professional conduct and attitude
  • Commitment to development and training

Panel Responsibilities

Want to Progress to a Higher Panel?

Interested in moving up? Here's what to do:

  1. Discuss your goals with the Coaching & Development officer
  2. Review the standards and requirements for your target panel
  3. Create a development plan with clear milestones
  4. Request regular assessments to track progress
  5. Attend all relevant training sessions
  6. Seek mentoring from panel members above you
Contact Coaching & Development

Panel Updates

Panel compositions are reviewed at the start of each season and may be updated throughout the year based on performance and development. For questions about panel placements, contact the Coaching & Development officer.
