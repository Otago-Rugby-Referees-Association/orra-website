Speak Freely, Anonymously

The ORRA Honesty Box provides a safe, anonymous way to ask questions, share concerns, or provide feedback to the executive committee. Your identity is completely protected.

What is the Honesty Box?

The Honesty Box is a confidential submission system where you can:

  • Ask Questions: Any question about ORRA, policies, procedures, or operations
  • Share Concerns: Raise issues that you might not feel comfortable discussing openly
  • Provide Feedback: Suggest improvements or share thoughts on how ORRA operates
  • Report Issues: Alert the executive to problems that need attention
  • Request Clarification: Get answers about decisions, appointments, or processes

üîí Your Privacy is Protected

  • Completely Anonymous: Google Forms doesn't collect email addresses or identifying information
  • No Tracking: We cannot see who submitted what
  • Secure: Only the executive committee can access submissions
  • Confidential: Submissions are treated with discretion

Note: While submissions are anonymous, please be respectful and constructive in your feedback.

How It Works

  1. Fill out the form below with your question, concern, or feedback
  2. Submit anonymously - no login or email required
  3. Executive reviews all submissions regularly
  4. Responses posted to common questions on this page or via email newsletter
  5. Action taken on issues that require executive attention

What Happens to Submissions?

Review Process

The executive committee reviews submissions weekly. Urgent matters are addressed immediately.

Responses

Common questions and their answers are posted in the FAQ section below. Individual responses are sent if you provided contact details.

Action Items

Issues requiring action are discussed at executive meetings and addressed appropriately.

Frequently Asked Questions from Honesty Box

Note: This section will be updated with common questions and answers from submissions. Check back regularly for updates.

Example Q&A

Q: "How are match appointments decided?"

A: Match appointments are made by the Appointments Officer based on referee level, availability, workload balance, development opportunities, and match requirements. If you have concerns about your appointments, please contact the Appointments Officer directly.

Example Q&A

Q: "Can I request feedback on my recent performances?"

A: Absolutely! Contact the Coaching & Development officer to arrange a feedback session or request a formal assessment. We encourage all referees to seek regular feedback.

Alternative Contact Methods

If you prefer to discuss matters directly rather than anonymously:

  • Email: Contact any executive member directly
  • In Person: Speak with executive members at training sessions or matches
  • Phone: Call the Chairperson or relevant officer
  • Formal Meeting: Request a meeting with the executive

We Value Your Input

ORRA is your association. We're committed to transparency, continuous improvement, and creating the best possible environment for our referees. Your honest feedback helps us achieve that goal.

Thank you for taking the time to share your thoughts!
